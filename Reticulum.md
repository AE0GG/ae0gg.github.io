# Reticulum
Google AI Overview
> Reticulum is a decentralized, open-source networking stack designed to build secure, resilient, and independent communication systems that operate with or without the internet. It enables diverse hardware—such as LoRa radios, Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and serial connections—to form a unified, self-organizing mesh network
