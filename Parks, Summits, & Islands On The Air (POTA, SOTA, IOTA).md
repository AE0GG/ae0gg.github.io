# Parks, Summits, & Islands On The Air (POTA, SOTA, IOTA)
Google AI Overview - POTA
> Parks on the Air (POTA) is a popular international amateur radio program where licensed operators set up portable stations in national, state, and local parks to make radio contacts with others. It promotes emergency communication skills, outdoor activity, and environmental awareness, with participants either "activating" a park or "hunting" them from home.

Google AI Overview - SOTA
> Summits on the Air (SOTA) is an international amateur radio award program that encourages portable, battery-powered radio operation from mountain summits. Participants ("activators") hike to designated peaks and make radio contacts with others ("chasers"), earning points based on summit height and season. It merges hiking with ham radio, launched in 2002.

Google AI Overview - IOTA
> Islands On The Air (IOTA) is a popular, long-running (since 1964) amateur radio awards program designed to promote radio contacts with stations operating from islands worldwide. Administered by IOTA Ltd in partnership with the Radio Society of Great Britain (RSGB), it encourages "chasers" to contact, and "activators" to transmit from, over 1,200 designated island groups.
