# High Frequency (HF)
Google AI Overview
> High Frequency (HF) radio refers to radio waves in the 3 to 30 MHz range, commonly used for long-distance (intercontinental) communication, amateur radio, and broadcasting. It relies on ionospheric skywave propagation to bounce signals off the atmosphere, allowing communication without infrastructure.
