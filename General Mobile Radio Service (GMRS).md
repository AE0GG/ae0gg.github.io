# General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS)
Google AI Overview
>The General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS) is a licensed, UHF-based, two-way radio service in the US, designed for personal, family, and recreational use, such as hiking or road trips. It provides higher power and longer range than walkie-talkies (FRS), allowing for mobile, handheld, and repeater usage over several miles.

## GMRS Licensing
1. [Register for an FCC Registration Number (FRN)](https://apps2.fcc.gov/fccUserReg/pages/createAccount.htm)
2. [Log into the ULS Online Filing System](https://wireless2.fcc.gov/UlsEntry/licManager/login.jsp)
3. [Apply for a New License](https://wireless2.fcc.gov/UlsEntry/licManager/narli/radioservice.jsp?action=actionNew)
   - Select **ZA - General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS)**
   - Submit the application fee ($35)
4. Within 24-72 hours, check the ULS Database for your license.
> [!IMPORTANT]
> You are not permitted to speak on the air until your license is in the ULS Database!
